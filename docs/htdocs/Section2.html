Emotions Connect Us

Music is a vessel for emotion, as discussed in a previous section. Emotion is felt by every single human being, regardless of age, backround, and experiences. It is what connects us all. When one is feeling a certain emotion, we can sympathize, and put ourselves in their shoes to have an idea of what they are feeling. You could share the joy, the sorrow, the excitement, and the grief with others, while being portrayed by the essence of music.


"Toothless Found" HTTYD OST by John Powell on YouTube

Here is one of the numerous amazing pieces from the soundtrack of the movie How to Train Your Dragon 2 by John Powell. It's a powerful moment where when all seems lost, the tides are miraculously turned, and our hero saves the day. This music piece captures and scores the moment very well.
