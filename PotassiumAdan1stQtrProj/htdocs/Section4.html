Context is Key

Communities live by context. A music piece doesn't need to be amazing to be considered special. Music can touch and affect a lot of people just by the context alone. It can bring people together in an ultimate conclusion: a community. Communities born this way, made by the hearts of the people who comprise it, stay immortal and is forever loved. Here is an utterly amazing example.

Philippine's "Lupang Hinirang" isn't really too astounding on its own, but with the context of a whole country, pouring their hearts into this song, to feel every Filipino's hearts sing as one, is a bewildering sight that no song without context could ever amount to.
