Building a Community

Emotions connect people. Oftentimes, when enough people are moved and touched by such emotion in the same way, communities form. Groups of people who can bond, interact, and be with people who have the same experiences as you did. If you are part of a community, you know those in the community have around the same passions as you. Of course, a lot of communities are supported by music.


"Five Nights at Freddy's" by TheLivingTombstone on YouTube

This is a song by TheLivingTombstone. This may not be groundbreaking, amazing, or intricaate, but it is a staple in the Five Night's at Freddy's Community. In the context of the community, many people in it enjoy the song as a community, unanimously loving it for the song that brought them together.
